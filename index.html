WELCOME!

Close to the beach

Just 18km from the bustling Bundaberg CBD awaits a haven of unspoilt nature where you, your family and pets can truly relax. Welcome to Moore Park Beach Motel! We'd like to invite you to sea, sun and sand coupled with an incomparable level of service that has earned us stellar reviews on TripAdvisor and Google. It's our privilege to provide both comfort and affordability to our guests but the Moore Park Beach Motel experience goes far beyond that. Stay with us and you'll enjoy:

Breathtaking sunsets and sunrises with 17km of north-east facing shoreline that's perfect for a romantic stroll
A proudly pet-friendly environment where your furry family members are lovingly welcomed
A peaceful environment at a boutique motel with only seven rooms that's easy to access from the heart of Bundaberg
Complimentary use of the Motel BBQ for making your favourite grilled specialty outdoors
Access to our relaxing salt-water pool and spa to let the stress melt away
A lovely seasonal breakfast menu full of fresh local produce.

Whether you're looking for somewhere to rest while travelling, you've had a medical visit to Bundaberg and need to convalesce or you just need a pleasant change of scenery, feel free to stay with us! Send us a message or give us a call and book your stay today.

Pet Friendly

The perfect holiday isn't perfect if your furrier family members can't come along too. That's why pets are welcome at Moore Park Beach Motel. Just let us know when you book your stay so we can prepare in advance

Enjoy The Sunset

Moore Park Beach's 17km of pristine coast is the perfect backdrop for a solitary or romantic stroll. You'll be tempted to wake up early to watch the sunrise and return later in the afternoon for gorgeous sunsets too

Comfortable Rooms

Our cosy motel hosts just seven rooms so there's never a crowd and you can relax in peace. All of our rooms are well maintained and offer a range of creature comforts you can enjoy throughout your stay.
