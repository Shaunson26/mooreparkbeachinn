Just 18 km from the bustling Bundaberg CBD awaits a haven of unspoilt nature where you, your family and pets can truly relax

Welcome to Moore Park Beach Inn! We'd like to invite you to sea, sun and sand coupled with an incomparable level of service that has earned us stellar reviews on TripAdvisor and Google. It's our privilege to provide both comfort and affordability to our guests but the Moore Park Beach Inn experience goes far beyond that. Stay with us and you'll enjoy:

  • Breathtaking sunsets and sunrises with 17 km of north-east facing shoreline that's perfect for a romantic stroll
  • A proudly pet-friendly environment where your furry family members are lovingly welcomed
  • A peaceful environment at a boutique motel with only seven accomodation rooms that's easy to access from the heart of Bundaberg
  • Complimentary use of the Inn BBQ for making your favourite grilled specialty outdoors
  • Access to our relaxing salt-water pool and spa to let the stress melt away
  • A lovely breakfast menu available to start your day

    • Whether you're looking for somewhere to rest while travelling, you've had a medical visit to Bundaberg and need to convalesce or you just need a pleasant change of scenery, feel free to stay with us! Send us a message or give us a call and book your stay today.

    Pet Friendly

    The perfect holiday isn't perfect if your furrier family members can't come along too. That's why pets are welcome at Moore Park Beach Inn. Just let us know when you book your stay so we can prepare in advance and please see our pet policy.

    Enjoy The Sunset

    Moore Park Beach's 17 km of pristine coast is the perfect backdrop for a solitary or romantic stroll. You'll be tempted to wake up early to watch the sunrise and return later in the afternoon for gorgeous sunsets too.

    Comfortable Rooms

    Our cosy Inn hosts just seven rooms so there's never a crowd and you can relax in peace. All of our rooms are well maintained and offer a range of creature comforts you can enjoy throughout your stay.
